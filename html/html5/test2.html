Thursday, June 21, 2012

6:30 PM

Engauge

Suite 2200, 1230 Peachtree Street, Atlanta, GA (edit map)

**Note - This meeting will be on the 2nd floor. We'll have tables where you can use your laptop to follow along.

Doors open at 6:30pm for refreshments and networking. Talk starts at 7.

About the presentation:

This is a hands-on introduction for those who are new to HTML5. It will cover what is HTML5, the basics, techniques you can use today and best practices. You are welcome to bring your own laptop and follow along.

About the speaker:

Toby likes to tinker, make cool stuff, and learn new things, and he blogs occasionally.
