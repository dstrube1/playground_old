Deprecated APIProperties | Methods | Styles
 

The evolution of ActionScript has deprecated many elements of the language. This section lists the deprecated items and suggests alternatives when available. While deprecated elements still work, Macromedia recommends that you do not continue using deprecated elements in your code. Support of deprecated elements in the future is not guaranteed.
